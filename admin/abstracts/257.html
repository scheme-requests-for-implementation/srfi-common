Pattern matching extends Scheme's repertoire of conditional constructs, allowing decomposition of compound data structures and binding their parts to variables. This SRFI proposes one such construct, match, which offers all the core functionality specified in SRFI 200, while extending it with support for non-linear patterns and backtracking.
